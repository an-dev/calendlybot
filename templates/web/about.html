    About

    What is this?

    RoomCompare helps people to find the best flatshare ad on spareroom. This free online service allows you to compare different ads side by side so you can make a weighted decision hassle-free.

    It all started when a friend was searching for yet another flat in London. Before long he had a whole bunch of tabs open on different spareroom tabs. Lots of things to consider for each one: price, location, current flatmates... So we figured, why not use a spreadsheet to track all of the information and check what are the best options?

    As software developers, we decided to build a product to help solve this problem - even better than writing things down on a spreadsheet as that would still take a lot of time. Just like that, RoomCompare was born.
