Frequently Asked Questions

Who is behind this?

Calenduck was created by Andy Idiaghe.

Is it secure?

We use OAuth and we do not share any of your contacts with third parties or other entities unless it's necessary for extra functionality.
Read more on our Privacy Policy.

Why does it cost money?

Subscription fees pay for server costs and development of new features.

I am not receiving event notifications!

Calenduck relies on Calendly to send notifications when events are scheduled or cancelled.
Please check their status page for more info or contact us.

How do I request a feature?

Contact the developer or give us a shout on twitter @calenduckapp.
We'd love to hear from you.
